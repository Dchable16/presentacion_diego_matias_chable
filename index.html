The Flag of Mexico

By: Diego Matías Chablé Sánchez

The flag is one of the three National Symbols of our country and represents us throughout the world.

Meaning of the Colours:

Green:

Hope for the future.

Meaning of the Colours:

White:

Unity and peace among Mexicans.

Meaning of the Colours:

Red:

The blood and valour of the heroes of our history.

The National Coat of Arms:

The national emblem depicts an eagle perched on a prickly pear cactus while devouring a snake.

Fun Facts:

We celebrate it on the 24th of February.

Fun Facts:

In 2008, it was voted on the internet as the most beautiful flag in the world.
